Feeding Forward

Node, EJS, MongoDB, Mongoose, HTML/CSS, Materialize
A full stack app that connects restaurants to foodbanks in Washington State to donate excess food that would otherwise add to the growing food waste problem. Built in Node.js, I used MongoDB to create user and items schemas and routing, and integrated Google Maps Api. Full CRUD was integrated to allow users to save, update and delete their donation items and food banks of their choice.

MentorSpace

React, MongoDB, Mongoose, HTML/CSS, Materialize
Responsive app that connects students with professionals who can sign up as mentors to give advice to students in their areas of expertise. Built in React, I integrated MongoDB and Mongoose routing and mentor and student schemas. Using Materialize, I implemented UI design, while my teammate integrated Zip Code API to allow students to search for mentor in their area.
